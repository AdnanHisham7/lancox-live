About LANCOX FZCO

A legacy of excellence in industrial trading and global supply chain management

Bridging Excellence Globally

LANCOX FZCO is a premier industrial trading company headquartered in Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai. We specialize in sourcing, supplying, and distributing high-quality industrial equipment, tools, and materials to key sectors including Oil & Gas, Energy, Construction, Manufacturing, and Petrochemical industries.

Our strategic location in Dubai positions us at the crossroads of global trade, enabling us to efficiently serve clients across the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and beyond. With a commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking world-class industrial solutions.

Through strategic partnerships with leading global manufacturers and a deep understanding of regional market needs, LANCOX FZCO ensures that our clients receive not just products, but comprehensive solutions that drive operational excellence and business growth.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred industrial trading partner in the region, recognized globally for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer excellence.
